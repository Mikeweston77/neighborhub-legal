Who We Are

Waterfall 3 is a residential community in South Africa, and NeighborHub helps bring the neighborhood closer together. Residents use the app to share safety alerts, report local issues, chat, organise events, browse local listings, vote on community polls, receive newsletters, and stay informed about committee updates.

🚨

Safety & Emergency Alerts

Receive instant community alerts and share incident reports with photos and location details.

📝

Report It

Log potholes, vandalism, fly-tipping, abandoned vehicles, and other local issues from your phone.

🤝

Wellness Checks

Polls & Newsletters

Vote on community matters and stay updated with announcements from administrators and committee members.
