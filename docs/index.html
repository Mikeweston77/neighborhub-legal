About

What Is NeighborHub?

NeighborHub is a mobile application designed for South African residential estates and homeowners associations. It brings residents and HOA committees together through a single secure platform — covering neighbourhood safety, community communication, event planning, a local marketplace, and subscription management.

Features

Everything Your Community Needs

NeighborHub combines neighbourhood watch tools, social communication, and HOA administration into one app.

🚨

Emergency Alerts

Instant push-notification and WhatsApp emergency alerts keep the entire community informed of safety incidents in real time.

💬

Community Chat

Hyperlocal group messaging, direct messages, and announcement channels for verified residents only.

📅

Events & RSVP

Create neighbourhood events, track RSVPs, and share updates — all managed directly in the app.

🛒

Local Marketplace

Buy, sell, and share items within the community. A trusted closed marketplace for verified neighbours.

🏘

HOA Management

Committee members manage subscriptions, resident approvals, notices, polls, and financials from an admin dashboard.

👮

Neighbourhood Watch

Digital patrol schedules, incident reporting, crime trend Heat maps, and a volunteer coordination system.

🔔

Pinned Notices

Admins can pin important announcements so no critical information gets lost in the feed.

💳

Subscriptions

Track household membership subscriptions with single and household plan options, payment history, and overdue alerts.
