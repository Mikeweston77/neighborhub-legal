NeighborHub - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to NeighborHub ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for NeighborHub or use the App, we may collect:

  • Name: Your full name for account setup and community identification
  • Email Address: For account creation, login, and communication
  • Contact Number: For support, verification, and optional account recovery or emergency-related features
  • Address or Residence Details: To verify neighborhood residency and eligibility for community access
  • Profile Photo: If you choose to upload one
  • Verification Details: Information submitted or reviewed during neighborhood/admin verification

Location Information

We may collect GPS location data while you use the App to:

  • Display local weather information
  • Support neighborhood verification and local community features
  • Show nearby events, services, and business search results
  • Support safety-related and neighbourhood watch functionality where enabled
  • Location Frequency: Only when you allow the App to access location while using it
  • You Control It: You can disable location services in iOS Settings

Content You Create

We also collect content you create in the App, including:

  • Messages: Community chat messages, replies, and reactions
  • Posts: Incident reports, event posts, marketplace listings, polls, and neighbourhood watch updates
  • Media: Photos, videos, voice messages, and documents you upload
  • Interactions: RSVPs, poll votes, business searches, and similar activity

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features you use, screens you view, and time spent in the App
  • Diagnostics: Crash reports, performance data, and error logs used for stability and troubleshooting

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide Core Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify you're a neighborhood resident
  • Enable community chat and messaging
  • Process incident reports, events, marketplace listings, polls, and neighbourhood watch activity
  • Display local businesses, weather information, and nearby services

2. Personalization

  • Show relevant local weather and news
  • Recommend nearby businesses and local content
  • Customize your experience based on preferences

3. Communication

  • Send push notifications for messages, alerts, and updates
  • Email notifications for account activity
  • Emergency alerts and safety notifications

4. Safety and Security

  • Verify user identities
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Moderate content for community safety
  • Respond to emergency or safety-related requests where available

5. Analytics and Improvement

  • Understand how users engage with features
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve app performance
  • Develop new features

6. Payments and Billing

  • Process eligible purchases and subscriptions
  • Support transaction records and billing-related support requests
  • Help prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Your Information With:

Other Neighborhood Members:

  • Your name and profile photo are visible to verified community members
  • Content you post (messages, events, listings) is visible to the community
  • Your location is never shared with other users

Within the App:

  • Content visible to verified community members remains visible within the App as intended
  • Neighborhood-watch and other community safety content may be shown to approved users according to app permissions

Service Providers:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud hosting, authentication, database, storage, analytics
  • Apple: Push notifications (APNs)
  • PayStack: Payment processing for eligible purchases and subscriptions
  • OpenWeatherMap: Weather data (location coordinates only), if used

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing outside the app

Required Disclosures:

We may disclose your information if required by:

  • Law enforcement or legal obligations
  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Emergency situations involving safety or security

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, we remove or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, subject to legal and operational requirements
  • Content: Messages and posts you created may remain visible in an anonymized form (for example, as "[Deleted User]")
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes

Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right To:

1. Access: Request a copy of your personal data

2. Correction: Update or correct your information in Settings

3. Deletion: Delete your account and associated data

4. Opt-Out: Disable push notifications, location services, or analytics

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Update Profile: Go to Settings → Edit Profile
  • Delete Account: Use the home tab user settings to delete your account, or contact support for assistance
  • Contact Us: Send email to neighbourhubapp@gmail.com

Location Services:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → NeighborHub
  • Choose: "Never," "Ask Next Time," or "While Using the App"

Push Notifications:

  • iOS Settings → Notifications → NeighborHub
  • Toggle off specific notification types

Payments

Payments for eligible purchases or subscriptions are processed through PayStack. We may share necessary transaction information with PayStack to complete payment processing, prevent fraud, and support billing-related inquiries. We do not directly store full card details on our servers.

Diagnostics and Analytics

We use analytics and crash reporting to understand how the App is used, improve reliability, diagnose issues, and develop new features. This may include feature usage data, performance information, and error reports.

Children's Privacy

NeighborHub is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication
  • Firestore Security Rules: Database access controls
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Storage Security: Cloud Storage access restrictions
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews

However, no system is 100% secure. Use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses these third-party services:

Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Services: Authentication, Firestore Database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Analytics, Crashlytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Data Processing: Data stored on Google Cloud servers (US region)

Apple

  • Services: Push Notifications (APNs), Location Services
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

OpenWeatherMap

  • Services: Weather data API
  • Privacy Policy: https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy
  • Data Shared: GPS coordinates (no personal information)

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

Contact: neighbourhubapp@gmail.com with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Erasure: "Right to be forgotten"
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Consent: For optional features like location services
  • Contract: To provide the services you request
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics and security

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted:

  • In the App (notification on next launch)
  • On our website (if applicable)
  • Via email for significant changes

Your Continued Use: Constitutes acceptance of updated policy
