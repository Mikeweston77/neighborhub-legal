Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Browse the questions below before reaching out — you may find your answer here.

How do I create an account?
Download the NeighborHub app and tap Sign Up. Enter your name, email address, and residential address. Your account will be reviewed and verified by the Waterfall 3 HOA committee before you gain full access to the community features.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it?
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your registered email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. If the email does not arrive, check your spam folder or contact us at mrcsweston@outlook.com.
My account is pending verification — what should I do?
Verification is done manually by the HOA committee. If it has been more than 2 business days since you registered, please contact us via email or WhatsApp and we will expedite your approval.
How do I report a neighbourhood incident?
Open the Watch tab and tap Add Incident. Fill in the details, attach a photo if available, and submit. Incident reports are visible to verified residents and the HOA committee.
How do I update my profile or contact details?
Go to your Profile tab, tap Edit Profile, make your changes, and save. If you need to update your registered address, please contact the HOA committee directly.
I'm having trouble with push notifications.
On your iPhone, go to Settings → Notifications → NeighborHub and make sure notifications are enabled. If the problem persists, try logging out of the app and back in. Still not working? Email us at mrcsweston@outlook.com.
How do I delete my account and data?
To request account deletion and removal of your personal data, email mrcsweston@outlook.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address. We will process the request within 7 business days in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
Is NeighborHub available on Android?
NeighborHub is currently available on iOS. Android support is planned for a future release. Join the community and we'll notify residents when the Android version launches.
